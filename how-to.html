How to use!

To begin there are a series of buttons at the bottom of the UI. THese buttons simulate Gestures, multiclicks, etc. that do not exist for the wall or no touchscreen devices. In the true UI they would NOT exist. this is kind of important to mention. to begin a shower there are 2 methods: Method 1. directly interface with the shower control unit Method 2. select the user from the dropdown and press pick user, then hit the scanner the scanner combined with the scale in the bottom of the tub allows individual users to be identified the other buttons serve to open the clock (to display time), show the profile selection menu for predefined custom selections, or reopen the controls menu should the user choose to close it the controls menu allows you to control all shower related functions. the four corner buttons are as follows upper left hand: closes menu upper right hand: modifies current profile with current settings lower left hand: opens drain (uneccessary as we don't have a drain) lower right hand: enables dangerously hot temperatures (safety first) the d pad in the center controls the angle of the shower head the sliders control the flow rate and termperature. if the flow slider is set to zero the system shutsdown the the toggle button lets you select what is currently producing water. finally we have the panic button which opens all toggleable menus (in case you lose your menus or the touchscreens begin malfunctioning) and shuts off water.
